Cloudiness

Though it is very difficult to find a consistent relationship between the amount of clouds and the latitude it is noticable that 0-30 degrees latitude in southern hemispheres leads to 40 - 50% cloud cover there. Also, it is noteworthy to mention that 40-70 latitude in northern hemisphere has the high concentration of clouds above 80%.
